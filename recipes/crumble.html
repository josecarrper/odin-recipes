Crumble

Description

This cherry crumble recipe is delicious served warm with vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1 1/8 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 6 tablespoons packed brown sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Remove from heat, then stir in flour, oats, brown sugar, and salt until mixture resembles dry crumbs. Press about 2/3 of the crumbs evenly into the bottom of a 9-inch square pan. Top with cherry pie filling and sprinkle with remaining crumb mixture.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is lightly browned, 40 to 45 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.
